Bitcoin hits $50k level for first time in more than two years

Bitcoin hit the $50,000 level for the first time in more than two years as the world's largest cryptocurrency was buoyed by expectations of interest rate cuts later this year and last month's regulatory nod for U.S. exchange-traded funds designed to track its price.

SaaS startup partners Boston Dynamics to deploy dog robot at construction sites

Zepth, a Gurugram-based software provider for construction management, partnered with Boston Dynamics to deploy the US company’s dog robot, ‘Spot’, at construction projects. It can collect data and facilitate artificial intelligence-led decision-making using Zepth’s analytics.

NFTFN selected for Pivot's Web3 acceleration program

NFTFN a web3 investment platform and decentralised exchange, has secured a sport in Pivot's flagship Web 3.0 acceleration program. Pivot is a global Web3 venture accelerator that supports companies in navigating the complex Web 3.0 landscape.

Russia to limit only VPN services which pose a 'threat' to security

Russia plans to block certain Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) and protocols deemed by a commission of experts to pose a threat, state news agency RIA reported citing correspondence from the digital ministry. Demand for VPN services soared after Russia restricted access to some Western social media after President Vladimir Putin ordered troops into Ukraine in February 2022.
